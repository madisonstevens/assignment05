Paris, TX

Paris Texas is a scenic place.

There is a classic film (that I have not seen) called Paris Texas it was directed by Wim Winders. Essentially, Paris is a magical place filled with Texans and black dirt. Paris, TX is south of the Red Dirt River Valley in Southern Oklahoma and Northern Texas. It has a fair amount of tornadoes, one of which I survived. While in Paris, mostly I went to Walmart and Starbucks. That's all they had there. Sometimes I went to the little stores owned by locals, they were okay but too religious for me. In Paris I worked on a dairy farm treating sick cows, it was pretty rad. I drove a sweet 1997 Ford F150 to Texas and back to my home in Massachusetts. Texas was a great place and I recommend that you visit. Check out the eiffel tower if you're in Paris! Also go to Popeye's.
